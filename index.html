Tessera Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Tessera is a personal habit- and event-logging calendar app. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it lives, and your choices.

Summary

  • Local-first. All your logging data is stored on your device. You can use every feature of Tessera without creating an account.
  • Optional sync. If you create an account, your data is synced to our backend (Supabase) so it can follow you across devices.
  • No ads, no data sales. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Data the app stores

On your device (always):

  • Activities you define (names, icons, fields, targets)
  • Daily logs and field values you enter
  • Journal entries, including any images you attach
  • App preferences (theme, display settings)

This data is stored in the app's private storage and is not accessible to other apps.

On our servers (only if you sign in):

  • Your email address (used as your account identifier)
  • The same activities, logs, journal text, and journal images as above, synced so they are available on your other devices

Sync data is stored with Supabase. Every record is scoped to your account: row-level security ensures no other user can read or modify your data. Journal images are stored in a private bucket accessible only to your account via short-lived signed URLs.

Crash reports (release builds only):

If the app crashes, a technical crash report (stack trace, device model, OS version) may be sent to Sentry to help us fix bugs. Crash reports are configured to exclude personally identifying information; your logging data is never included.

What we do NOT collect

  • No analytics or behavioural tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos (other than images you explicitly attach to journal entries), or other device data

Data retention and deletion

  • Local data stays on your device until you delete it (Settings → Clear logging history) or uninstall the app.
  • Account deletion is available in-app: Settings → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account, all synced records, and all uploaded images from our servers. It cannot be undone. Data on your device is kept unless you clear it separately.
  • Sign out stops syncing but keeps both local and server data intact.

Delete your Tessera account

To delete your account and all data associated with it (activities, logs, journal entries, and uploaded images):

  1. Open Tessera and go to Settings → Delete account, then confirm. Deletion is immediate and permanent.
  2. If you can no longer access the app, email simplegenius351@gmail.com from the email address on your account with the subject “Delete my Tessera account”. Requests are honoured within 30 days.

In both cases every synced record and image tied to your account is permanently removed from our servers. Data stored locally on your device is unaffected until you clear it or uninstall the app.

Children

Tessera is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.
